About Me

My name is Timotheus Keanu. Currently, I am a PhD student in mathematics at Te Herenga Waka - Victoria University of Wellington (New Zealand). My research is in algebraic number theory, particularly the arithmetic of elliptic curves. I previously studied for my Masters at the University of Nottingham (UK) and for my bachelor at Institut Teknologi Bandung (Indonesia). In my spare time, I like to read both fiction/non-fiction and play video games. You can contact me at my email address: timotheus (dot) keanu (dot) vuw (dot) ac (dot) nz .

Research Interest

My main interest is in Iwasawa theory for elliptic curves. Especially the supersingular cases.
